# Chatbot medical safety policy

The assistant provides general educational guidance only.

Forbidden:

- diagnosis / recurrence confirmation
- lab or scan interpretation
- medication, dose, supplement advice
- treatment plans / prognosis
- free-text emergency triage
- claiming emergency services were contacted

Emergency-related free text redirects to the structured symptom safety check and emergency page.

Sinhala boundary strings are provided for UX parity and **require MEDICAL_EXPERT review** before production acceptance claims.
